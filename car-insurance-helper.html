2 · Autofill button

Drag this button to your bookmarks bar. Then, on any quote form you've opened, click it to paste your saved details into matching fields. You still solve any CAPTCHA and submit yourself — it's a typing aid, not a bot.

⤓ Insurance Autofill

How it works & limits

The button carries your saved profile inline and best-effort matches each field by its label, name, placeholder or aria-label (e.g. a field mentioning "eircode" gets your Eircode). It won't beat every site — forms vary, and some load fields in steps — so always eyeball what it filled before submitting. Re-drag the button after editing your profile to refresh the data it carries. It only fills fields; it never clicks submit, and it sends nothing anywhere.
